Page 5: Wildlife

The country is truly diverse, possessing three ecosystems and three national parks within 10,000 square miles. Home to rare mountain gorillas, lions, rhinoceroses, elephants, giraffes, zebras, over 700 bird species, and so much more, visiting Rwanda should be at the top of any wildlife lover's bucket list.

Other wildlife includes Maasai giraffe, Burchell's zebra, warthog, olive baboon, vervet monkey, hippo, impala, Defassa waterbuck, bushbuck, common duiker, eland, topi, Bohor reedbuck, oribi, roan antelope, klipspringer and the secretive semi-aquatic sitatunga.
